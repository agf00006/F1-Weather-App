Formula 1 Tire Choices

C0
- Hardest tire in the range, maximum reststance to heat and force
C1
- 2nd hardest tire, designed to reduce the gap between compounds
C2
- 3rd hardest tire, suited for hot, abrasive surfaces
C3
- Extremely versatile with an excellent balance
C4
- 2nd softest tire, designed for low-severity circuits and fast warm-up
C5
- Softest tire in the range, designed for slow circuits with low wear
Intermediate
- Most versatile of the rain tires, can be used on both a wet and drying surface
Full Wet
- Most effective for heavy rain, designed to disperse water
